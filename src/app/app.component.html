My name is Diego and I am a multidisciplinary artist, graphic designer and illustrator based in Madrid, Spain.

I’ve been doing work for high profile clients and top brands around the world with my personal signature ‘Paranoidme’ since 2009.

Heavily influenced by cinema, art nouveau and a mixture of ancient and contemporary cultures, my work is imaginative and detailed, featuring unique color palettes, forms and sometimes (why not), abstraction.
